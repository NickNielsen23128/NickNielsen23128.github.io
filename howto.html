HOW TO PLAY

Guess the FRONTENDLE in six tries. Not all words are dictionary words, but they all have to do with frontend development.


Each guess must have the same number of letters as there is boxes. Hit the enter button to submit your guess.


After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess is to the corect word.
